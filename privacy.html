Privacy Policy

Effective date: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Zaela LLC ("we", "us") handles information in the MyoState mobile app (the "App").

The short version

Your entries are stored on your device, and the App has no account — nothing is filed under your name. Apple Health, if you connect it, is read-only and converted to simple ratings on your device — raw health values are never stored or transmitted. What we receive is limited: anonymous crash reports, anonymous feature-usage counts, and your subscription status.

Information stored on your device

The App has no account and does not ask for your name, email, or other contact details. The entries you create — workouts, exercise data, meal plans, daily 1–5 self-ratings, habit checkmarks, and preferences such as your protein and sleep targets — are stored locally on your device. If you import workout history from a file (for example, a CSV exported from another app), the file is read and processed entirely on your device. We do not collect these entries. Your device's own backup (such as iCloud device backup) may include the App's data under Apple's protections and your Apple ID.

Apple Health

If you choose to connect Apple Health, the App reads fitness data — sleep, dietary protein, dietary calories, and body weight — with your permission, which you can revoke at any time in the Health app. Health data is read-only: the App never writes to Apple Health. Sleep and nutrition values are converted on your device into simple 1–5 ratings (for example, hours slept becomes a sleep rating); the App stores only those ratings, never the underlying values, and ratings cannot be converted back into them. Body weight works differently: a reading from Health is only shown as a suggestion, and is saved — on your device, like a workout entry — only if you log it as a weigh-in; you can delete any weigh-in at any time. Nothing read from or derived from Apple Health ever leaves your phone, and it is never used for advertising, analytics, or marketing. Disconnecting Health affects future days only — apart from weigh-ins you chose to log, no raw history exists in the App to remove, because none was kept.

Services we use

Three narrow services support the App, none of which receive your training data, health data, or identity:

  • Crash reporting (Sentry): if the App crashes, a technical report (stack trace, device model, OS version) is sent so we can fix the bug. No personal or health information is included.
  • Usage analytics (TelemetryDeck): anonymous, aggregated event counts (for example, that a workout was logged — never its contents). TelemetryDeck is a privacy-first service and cannot identify you.
  • Purchases (RevenueCat): if you subscribe, your subscription status is processed under a random identifier so the App knows Pro is active. Payment itself is handled entirely by Apple.

    • Notifications

    All notifications are optional and scheduled locally on your device from your own settings: workout reminders, the daily check-in reminder, and — if you choose one when starting a free trial — a single reminder before the trial ends. We do not send push notifications from our servers, and notification content never leaves your device.

    Sharing

    We do not share your information. If you use the export or share features, you choose where that content goes. The operating system and app store you use may collect certain information under their own policies, which we do not control.

    Retention and deletion

    Your information stays on your device until you remove it. You can export or permanently erase all of it at any time in the App's settings. Erasing removes your data from the App immediately; crash and usage records already sent contain nothing capable of identifying you.

    Security

    Your information is protected by your device's own security features. You are responsible for keeping your device secure.

    Children

    The App is intended for users aged 13 and older. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

    Your rights

    Because your information stays on your device, you control it directly — view, export, or delete it within the App. You may have additional rights under laws such as the GDPR or CCPA; as we do not hold personal information on our side, there is generally nothing for us to provide or delete.

    Changes

    We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when the updated version is made available.
